Why SI Matters

The SI system provides several crucial advantages:

  • Universal Standard: Scientists worldwide use the same measurements, facilitating collaboration
  • Decimal System: Conversions are simple, using powers of 10
  • Fundamental Constants: Modern definitions based on nature's constants ensure permanence
  • Consistency: All units fit together logically
  • Precision: Definitions enable extremely accurate measurements

Global Governance

The SI system is maintained by the International Bureau of Weights and Measures (BIPM) in France. The General Conference on Weights and Measures (CGPM) meets regularly to review and update standards as science advances.
